How BreakBuddy Works

  • Set your work and break intervals
  • Start working and let BreakBuddy watch out for you
  • When work time ends, you'll get a friendly reminder
  • You can snooze the reminder twice if you're in the zone
  • After the third reminder, BreakBuddy will gently force you to rest

Press Ctrl+Shift+B to show BreakBuddy anytime!
